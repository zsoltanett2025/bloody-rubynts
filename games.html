BR Lost Isle 2D

A dark 2D adventure set in an 1800s-inspired world. The journey begins with a shipwreck and grows through survival, exploration, branching routes, collectibles, companions, and story progression.

  • Shipwreck opening and island survival
  • Characters, companions, and rescue objectives
  • Rune stones, keys, items, and branching paths
  • Unity-based development and custom systems
