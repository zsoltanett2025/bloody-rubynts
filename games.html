BR Lost Isle 2D Preview
In Development

BR Lost Isle 2D

A dark 2D adventure in an 1800s-inspired fictional world. The planned journey begins with a shipwreck and develops through survival, exploration, branching routes, companions, and story progression.

  • Shipwreck opening and island survival
  • Characters, companions, and rescue objectives
  • Rune stones, keys, items, and branching paths
  • Unity-based development and custom systems
In-game systems clarification: references to rewards, bonuses, balances, collectibles, shops, or progression on this page describe game-design systems. They do not promise BR cryptoasset payments, financial returns, exchangeable value, or future token integration.
