2. What these technologies are

Cookies are small files placed on a device. Local storage and session storage are browser features serving similar purposes. In this policy, “cookies” may refer collectively to these technologies where appropriate.

Necessary storage supports choices and basic website behaviour. Optional analytics helps us understand general website use and is activated only after you consent.

4. Google Analytics

Google Analytics is not loaded unless you select “Accept analytics”. If accepted, Google may receive online identifiers and technical or usage information, including browser and device details, pages viewed, interactions, referral information and approximate location.

Further information is available in Google’s Privacy Policy. See our Privacy Policy for more information about purposes, rights and international processing.

5. External links and technical requests

This website links to external music, store, social-media and blockchain services. Clicking a link opens the other provider’s service, where its own cookies and policies apply. Website hosting and content-delivery providers may also receive technical request data needed to deliver files, even when optional analytics is rejected.

6. Managing or withdrawing consent

Use the Cookie settings button in the footer at any time. Selecting “Reject analytics” disables analytics for the site and removes recognised Google Analytics cookies that this website can access. You may also delete stored information through your browser settings.

Blocking all browser storage may affect the website’s ability to remember your preference, so the consent question may appear again.

We may update this policy if website technology, providers or legal requirements change. For questions, email bloodyrubynts@gmail.com.
