How to Buy BR

BR can be acquired through a decentralised exchange on Polygon. Always verify the contract address before making a swap.

1. Prepare a Wallet

Install MetaMask or another compatible wallet. Never share your recovery phrase.

2. Select Polygon

Switch the wallet to Polygon and keep a small amount of POL for transaction fees.

3. Open a DEX

Use QuickSwap or another compatible Polygon decentralised exchange.

4. Swap POL/WPOL to BR

Paste the verified BR contract address, start with a small test amount, and confirm the swap.

Security: always compare the token contract with the official PolygonScan page. Token purchases involve market and smart-contract risk. No return, price increase, or profit is guaranteed.
