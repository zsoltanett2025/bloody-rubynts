Bloody Rubynts®

More than a name. A growing creative world built through music, visual identity, game development, storytelling, merchandise, imagination, and perseverance.

What Is Bloody Rubynts®?

Bloody Rubynts® is an independent creative project developed step by step. Music, artwork, games, merchandise, and future digital experiences are connected through one shared identity.

The Ruby

The ruby represents determination, creative strength, and the ability to continue moving forward through difficult times.

One Creative World

Music, games, visual artwork, merchandise, and storytelling are different parts of one growing Bloody Rubynts® ecosystem.

Music and Emotion

Every release carries its own atmosphere, story, emotion, and visual identity.

Games and Storytelling

Match-3, BR Lost Isle 2D, and future 3D projects extend the world through interaction, exploration, characters, and player choice.

Visual Identity

The Bloody Rubynts® visual identity uses a dark atmosphere, ruby-red light, black backgrounds, metallic details, and symbols connected to creativity, survival, mystery, and persistence.

Ruby Red

Energy, determination, emotion, and the central brand symbol.

Dark Contrast

Black and deep shadow create focus, atmosphere, and identity.

Strength

Endurance, protection, and forward movement.

Creative Freedom

Music, art, games, and merchandise can grow without losing the core identity.
