About Bloody Rubynts®

A growing independent creative world built through music, games, artwork, merchandise, storytelling, practical work, experimentation, and long-term perseverance.

The Bloody Rubynts® Story

One Idea, Many Connected Paths

Bloody Rubynts® began as more than a single product or release. The idea was to build one recognisable world in which music, visual identity, games, merchandise, stories, and possible future digital experiences could connect.

The project is developed independently and gradually. Some parts are public, while others remain in testing, redesign, or development. Work in progress is not presented as a guaranteed final product.

The past shaped today. What comes next is still being written.

How We Work

The project follows a practical process based on steady progress, review, testing, correction, and improvement.

Step by Step

Large ideas are divided into smaller working parts. Each completed part provides experience for the next stage.

Function Before Polish

Prototypes, draft artwork, and early systems are used to test whether an idea works before every visual detail is final.

Review and Improve

Code, artwork, music, interfaces, and written material can be corrected, replaced, or improved when stronger versions become available.

One Connected Creative World

Each area has its own purpose, while every part belongs to the same Bloody Rubynts® identity.

Music

Original releases, albums, artwork, distribution, and music merchandise.

Games

Match-3 development, BR Lost Isle 2D, systems, interactive-world concepts, and possible future experiments.

Brand

Registered identity, logos, symbols, visual direction, and official creative materials.

Merchandise

Available music-linked products and possible future collections subject to testing and availability.

Development Journey

The project develops through real work rather than a fixed finish line or guaranteed schedule.

Establish the Identity

Create the name, ruby symbol, visual direction, and foundation of a connected creative world.

Release and Test

Publish music, build game systems, test merchandise and presentation, and learn from practical results.

Improve Quality

Replace weaker assets, refine code, improve visuals, strengthen the website, and record corrections.

Expand Carefully

Consider additional designs, games, music, products, and community work only when there is a sufficiently stable base. These remain possibilities, not guaranteed releases.

Independent Development

Built Through Real Work

Bloody Rubynts® is independently financed, organised, developed, tested, and improved. Progress is made around work, family responsibilities, limited time, and practical constraints. This can make development slower than a large studio, while keeping the direction personal.

Long-Term Social Direction

The wider vision may eventually include carefully verified and goal-based practical support. No public support programme is currently operating, and no purchase, token acquisition, merchandise order, or community membership creates a right to assistance or guarantees that a programme will launch.

Creative Support

A possible future aim is practical support for early-stage musicians and creators through resources, mentoring, or visibility.

Families and Students

A future model may consider verified, goal-based assistance for people facing genuine difficulty.

Practical Help

If introduced, assistance would focus on documented needs and direct solutions rather than uncontrolled cash distribution.

Current status: these are long-term aims only. They are not active charitable services, fundraising claims, or promises that project income will be used for a particular beneficiary.
